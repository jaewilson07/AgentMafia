# Block Kit
A clean and consistent UI framework for Slack apps
Customize the order and appearance of information and guide users through your app's capabilities by composing, updating, sequencing, and stacking **blocks** — reusable components that work almost everywhere in Slack.
![](https://api.slack.com/img/api/block-kit/block_kit_splash.png)
## Block basics
### [Building blocks](https://api.slack.com/block-kit/building)
Blocks are visual components that can be stacked and arranged to create app layouts.
[Read our guide to learn how](https://api.slack.com/block-kit/building) you can construct stacks of blocks and add them to your app's surfaces.
### [Interactivity in blocks](https://api.slack.com/block-kit/building#making-things-interactive)
Use special components to inject an app with interactivity.
[Read our overview](https://api.slack.com/block-kit/interactivity) to get a quick taster of the interactive components available, and pointers on how to integrate interactivity into your app.
### [Build and prototype visually](https://api.slack.com/tools/block-kit-builder)
Shuffle and stack blocks to quickly prototype layouts in Slack. When you're ready, we'll provide the payload so all you have to do is copy and paste it into your app's code.
Design layouts with [Block Kit Builder](https://api.slack.com/tools/block-kit-builder). Or save time designing your app by starting with one of our [templates](https://api.slack.com/tools/block-kit-builder?template=1).
